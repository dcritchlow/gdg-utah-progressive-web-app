For all Familes

DevFest Fam is a tech conference for the whole family, whether you are a programmer or not! We have something for everyone in the family. 

Hands on Activities

From Rockets, to Legos, to coding. We have hands on activities that will keep the busiest kids entertained. With activities for everyone in the family of all ages, we can ensure a day of family fun. 

Learn New Skills

No one is too young or too old to learn new skills at DevFest fam. Whether you are coding for the first time, or just want to learn more about keeping your family safe on the internet, you will learn something new. 

Meet New People

DevFest Fam is full of people from all walks of life. From our attendees alone there are a variety of different backgrounds and specialties, our sponsors are great to rub shoulders with as well!

Families Who Code Together,
Stay Together

DevFest Fam is a Family Developer Conference hosted by the Utah Google Developer Group

Our goal is to introduce everyone to coding and help everyone become more familiar and comfortable with technology. For technical parents who want to sharpen their skills or find new ways to help teach their children to non technical parents who want to learn a new skill or help encourage their family members, to children who are being introduced to coding and technology for the very first time. DevFest Fam is the first conference focused on bringing something to the table for every family member. 

Would you like to speak at DevFest Fam? We have started our call for papers and would love for you to bring your knowledge to our event!
